CTU Information Corner

CTU

Cebu Technological University, a place that offers various and generous academic opportunities paired with innovative learning techniques, has become one of the well-known universities in Cebu. It offers a large variety of top class degree programs which will help individuals discover their career paths and help them achieve it. In particular, the CTU Danao campus offers a variety of facilities to complement the various courses and degree programs being offered. In this article, we'll delve into some of the noteworthy facilities in CTU Danao Campus

  • Kadasig Gym
  • Sports Complex
  • Hometel
  • Student Activity Center

Kadasig Gym

Named after the Cebuano word for enthusiasm and courage, Kadasig Gym is designed to inspire students to pursue basketball and futsal with passion and determination. The facility encourages the development of athletic skills while also serving the recreational needs of the CTU Danao community. Located in a spacious area alongside other sports facilities, Kadasig Gym features two basketball hoops and a stage, making it a versatile venue for both athletic events and performing arts productions, as well as other outdoor activities.

Ramon M. Durano Sr. Sports Memorial Sports Complex

This modern facility features a newly constructed 8-lane rubberized track, designed for university athletes and fitness enthusiasts in Danao City. Its versatile design allows it to host not only athletic events but also concerts, festivals, and other major gatherings. The complex is the venue for the annual Karansa Festival, Danao City's official celebration, and has also hosted prestigious events such as the Xterra Asia-Pacific Championship, making it a hub for both sports and cultural activities in the region.

CTU Danao Hometel

Escape the busy city life and unwind at this 16-room hidden gem nestled within the university. Offering a peaceful retreat, the Hometel provides stunning 360-degree views of the Northeastern Cebu landscape, ideal for those seeking a serene getaway. Guests can take a refreshing dip in the newly-installed swimming pool while enjoying the breathtaking sunrise over the eastern coastline. With its comfortable accommodations, welcoming atmosphere, and proximity to the university’s amenities, it’s perfect for visiting faculty, students, and travelers looking to explore the beauty of Cebu while enjoying a relaxing stay.

Student Activity Center (SAC)

The heart of the University's academic ceremonies and social and official functions are held in the SAC. This conventionally designed but fully-lighted and fully-air-conditioned activity center renders a dramatic transition between the contemporary CME, COE, and COED buildings to the accustomed CTU Danao configuration, and the linkage between the academic hub and the vibrant campus sports and cultural core. The student activity center features a cafe-style foyer for collaboration and socialization, connecting to a newly-established outdoor open space as dance and theater rehearsals and flexible meeting bays. Purposely built to host multiple campus programs, this 400-sqm facility also provides a new venue for university-wide gatherings. This center offers an indoor facility's convenience, wherein other stakeholders' need for space for celebrations and occasions is accommodated. This facility is a new step in bringing the academic, cultural, athletic departments, and the community closer together.
